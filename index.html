Welcome to ASANA

This application aims to introduce yoga to those who are interested by exposing them to these 5 basic forms.

This helps them to improve and ensure that their form is right, preventing injuries during practice.

Features:

  • Detection of 5 different poses
  • Detection of 3 different idles
  • Detection of 3 different equipments
  • A rating determining how well you are doing
  • AI voice audio
  • Calming music
